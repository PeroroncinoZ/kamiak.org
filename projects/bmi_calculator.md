To calculate BMI: $$\text{BMI}=\frac{\text{weight}}{\text{height}^2}$$
```
BMI    Evaluation
0-16   extremely underweight
16-18  underweight
18-25  healthy
25-30  overweight
30+    extremely overweight
```
